About

Taranjeet Singh is a first-year Multimedia Production program student at Lethbridge College. He was born and raised in New Delhi, India. He is 19 years old. He lives in a nuclear family with his parents, and one elder sibling. He really like to play Badminton. He is also good at playing the instrument named “Harmonium.” In his leisure time, he loves to go for a long walk and take pictures of the sky, clouds just calm his soul. In fact, he also sings sometimes. Since Childhood he was very keen to learn new things. He has a deep affection for Dogs as he also has one Pomeranian American Splitz, named Tuffy. Tuffy is extremely cute and innocent. Taran enjoys exploring novel places, hidden gems, and historical monuments. He loves to try out new outfits, new turban colors that make a nice combination. He always likes to try out new cuisines and explore diverse cultures. Supercars are one of the best things that intrigues him, the sound of supercars really fascinates him and motivates him to work hard in his life to buy one of them, one day. His ambition is to become a businessman and wants to run a designing studio, along with that, As a side hustle he wants to become a freelancer graphic designer and wants to invest in stock market, which will help him to get multiple income resources.
