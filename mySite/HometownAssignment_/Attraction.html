Several popular attractions of New Delhi are none other than Qutub Minar, Lotus temple, Red Fort, and India Gate. Rashtrapati Bhawan: residence of prime minister of India is a big attraction in New Delhi. Besides that, Palika Bazaar is a huge retail market of everything at affordable prices with more varieties in Cannaught place, New Delhi is the heart of attraction. Night clubs, nightlife, Concerts, Sneaker Events are also the best. There are so many things to explore in Delhi. The more you go the more you get to know. There is a famous street food shop near my house named “Rama Chole Batture” they make this north Indian dish unbelievably Delicious and the most exciting thing about this is that one of the most famous and successful Indian Cricket Player “Virat Kohli” has also tried this flavorsome food item. Chandni Chowk is another famed food tech gadgets and clothing market at affordable prices.
