Delhi people are known as Delhiites. They are very friendly and helpful. They are not at all rude. Truly diverse masses live in Delhi but with a Delhiite friendly attitude. Always available to help everyone. There is a famous saying in India “Atithi Devo Bhav” means Guests are like God, always carry very heart warming and welcoming personality. Folks in India always greet with their elders by touching their feet and take blessings from them. Additionally, they people also greet with the hand gesture by doing “Namaste”. Most of the people live in extended family even after their marriages, they live with their parents. As they believe to always be there with them in need of support and this also shows the unity and togetherness of families.
