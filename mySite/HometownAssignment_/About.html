New Delhi, National Capital of India. it is situated in the northern part of India. Delhi is undoubtedly one of the most amazing cities because of its cultural activities& events. There is a famous saying in India about New Delhi is “Dilli Dilwalo ki” it means, Delhi is the city of love and city of people with big hearts. In addition, there are well-known places to visit in this beautiful city like Appu Ghar, adventure parks and several types of markets in sub hub of Delhi, and they are not the ordinary markets, these markets are usually open till midnight and are according to the days like Monday market, Tuesday market, Wednesday market, and this continues till Sunday market. Infact, Malls are open till late light and on holidays till midnight. It is a metropolitan city that has all the facilities available. The Transit system is very convenient and on time too.
